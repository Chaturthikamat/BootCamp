SHIPPING OF CONTAINERS

Dangerous goods labeling of Intermodal containers

Vacuro - Really easy !!!

With Vacuro's patented danger holders, labeling of containers with dangerous goods makes it easy to play.
Glue-free danger tickets are inserted and removed from the holders in a hand turn.

The system therefore provides conveyors:
  • Time savings
  • cost savings
  • More secure labeling
  • environmental benefits

Why Vacuro?

Vacuro is composed of two Latin words.

Vacu, which means: "be empty" , "be free", "be open". Vacu describes the label holder itself - the label holders have free space for inserting danger labels.

Curo, which means: "slip easily" , "take care of", "make sure you find". Curo describes the function of the label holder - the label holders ensure that danger notes slip in and out of the holder very easily.
