About Vacuro

Vacuro markets and sells proprietary and patented holders for danger tickets and transport documents.

The holders ensure safe, very fast and cost-effective labeling of containers containing dangerous goods. The products are patented and design protected worldwide.

Our customers are all transporters that transport dangerous goods by road, rail and sea, as well as all container manufacturers. Our largest customers and partners nationally for several years are DB Schenker AS, Posten AS and Bring AS.

Background

The background to the development of the new concept for the labeling of dangerous goods was, as with many other new inventions, - the need to streamline and save money. The system also contributes to meeting the carriers' responsibility to comply with statutory safety regulations during the transport of dangerous goods. This is done by simplifying the job of labeling containers or other transport units.

Vacuro's holders have been tested for 7-8 years at DB Schenker in Norway, among others. The experience so far is very positive and DB Schenker, Posten and Bring are now equipping all their new material / containers with Vacuro holders.
